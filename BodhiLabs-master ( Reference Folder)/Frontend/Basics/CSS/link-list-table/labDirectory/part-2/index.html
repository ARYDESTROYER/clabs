Potion of Invisibility

Ingredients

  • One moon-kissed dewdrop from a silver leaf which must be gathered only on a Tuesday
  • Three scales of a shadow serpent
  • A whisper captured in a glass vial
  • Powdered horn of a mischievous pixie

Method

  1. Stir the dewdrop counter-clockwise under a new moon.
  2. Gently fold in the serpent scales one by one.
  3. Release the whisper into the mixture and seal the vial immediately.
  4. Let the potion rest in absolute darkness for seven days and seven nights before use.
